## **Installation**

### **Installation Steps**

1. Download the installation file from our official website: https://loic-1.github.io/Another-Recursion.github.io/index.html
2. Double-click the installation file and follow the on-screen instructions.
3. Once the installation is complete, launch the game via the icon created on your desktop, from the start menu or directly from
the installed executable

## **End of installation**
